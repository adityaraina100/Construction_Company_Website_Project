100 Bedded Boys Hostel at GMC

M/s JSCC has successfully executed the construction of a modern, 100-bedded boys' hostel at Government Medical College, Jammu. This thoughtfully crafted facility provides a secure and comfortable living environment, featuring expansive rooms and well-equipped commercial spaces. Designed with a focus on sustainability and innovative solutions, the hostel not only supports the academic journey of medical students but also contributes to their personal growth. This development exemplifies JSCC’s dedication to creating high quality , impactful spaces that enhance student life and educational experience.
